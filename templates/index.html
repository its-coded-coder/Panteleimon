ST. PANTELEIMON

Community based organisation

A home Of Love To Orphans, Vulnerable Children And Widows

Donate

Objectives

Organizing and carrying out charity work on the less fortunate children in our community, formulating projects that benefit the community and empowering the widows and Vulnerable people with disabilities.

Mission

To improve the lives of community by providing a safe place for vulnerable babies and young children is one of our main aims. At St. Panteleimon we believes that where possible children should be in a family,ideally their own. Therefore assisting families in adequate caring for their children is a huge part of our mission.

Vision

To provide a safe caring and loving environment for children (regardless of HIV status or disability) who are vulnerable, orphaned or abandoned.

Achievements

Having founded this prestigious mission in 2015, the orthodox community and other well wishers from different parts of the world have always stretched their merciful hands through their support. We have been able to provide education, shelter, clothes, food and healthcare to kids at the mission.

Contribution to humanity

The humble shall see God.

Some of our main objective are:

  • To organize and carry out charity work on the less fortunate children in our community.
  • To formulate projects that benefit the community.
  • To empower the widows and vulnerable people with disabilities

St. Panteleimon community based organisation mission's pioneer Mr Raphael Andanyi was born and raised up with quite humble and God fearing parents. He was raised in accordance with orthodox doctrines and was educated with support of Archbishop Makarios from elementary level to tertiary level. He was born alongside one sister three brothers, but surprisingly enough he had quite unique traits unlike the rest. It is clear his humble background strengthen his positive attitude towards supporting the vulnerable in the society and his characteristics reflects a man naturally made and created with Lord given love.


Service to humanity is one of the cornerstone whose bliss of positive attitude reflects the true character and the teachings of Lord and savior Jesus Christ. Having been brought up holistically by the Orthodox church motivated him to establish a mission center to help orphans, vulnerable children and widows as a way of giving back to the society.

EDUCATION

St Panteleimon community based organization is delighted to welcome you to our family. Our children focused programmes are designed to ensure that all children have a solid start to an an exciting future. All children are special and gifted with different talents and capabilities and we we strive to nurture each child individually moulding their social and moral attributes so that they can succeed in future. St Panteleimon academy is a learning centre that is determined to uphold educational quest to transform the lives of vulnerable children. The foundation of a child educational development is crucial.The academy will teach the children language, mathematics, life skills, social studies, art and craft, science and music.

class categories

  • Pre unity 5-6 years
  • Baby class 3-4 years
  • Nursery 4-5 years
  • Play group 2-3 years
Educational excellence is the main goal we strive to achieve.

We also believe that a wholesome mind needs physical stimulation of the body and engagement in various extra curricular activities for children round development, therefore we organize different sporting and extra curricular activities.
